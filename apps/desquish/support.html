DeSquish Support

Welcome to the support page for DeSquish, the anamorphic photo desqueeze app. If you're running into an issue or have a question, you'll likely find your answer below. If not, reach out directly at desquishphotoeditor@gmail.com.


Getting Started

DeSquish takes a squeezed anamorphic photo — one shot through an anamorphic adapter or lens on a mirrorless camera — and stretches it back to its correct proportions.

  • Import a squeezed photo from your camera roll
  • Choose your lens's squeeze ratio (1.33x, 1.5x, 1.8x, 2.0x) or enter a custom value
  • Preview the desqueeze update live as you adjust the ratio
  • Crop the result using the drag-and-resize handles
  • Save the finished photo straight back to your Photos library

Frequently Asked Questions

Which squeeze ratios does DeSquish support?
DeSquish includes presets for the most common anamorphic ratios — 1.33x, 1.5x, 1.8x, and 2.0x — and also supports a custom ratio if your lens uses a different value.


Does DeSquish upload my photos anywhere?
No. All processing happens on your device. Your photos never leave your phone, and DeSquish doesn't use accounts or cloud storage.


Can I desqueeze video with DeSquish?
Not yet. DeSquish is built for still photos first. Video support is on the roadmap as the app grows.


Why does my photo look slightly off after desqueezing?
Double check that the squeeze ratio you selected matches the ratio of your anamorphic adapter or lens. Using the wrong ratio is the most common cause of a result that looks stretched too much or too little.


Where is my desqueezed photo saved?
Once you save, the result is written directly to your device's Photos library, alongside your original.


I found a bug or have a feature request — where do I send it?
Email desquishphotoeditor@gmail.com with a description of the issue (and a screenshot if it's visual). Feature requests are always welcome.
